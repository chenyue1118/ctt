Questions and Answers on How to Book Tickets
Q: What information required when I book China train tickets?
A: The passenger’s correct ID number and full name are required. Double check when you submit the ID number, if the ID number and name are wrong, you can’t get the ticket with pick-up code or board the train. Valid ID number includes Passport for foreigners, Mainland travel Permit for Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan residents.
Q: When can I book train tickets?
A: There is no limitation on pre-booking. When you settle a travel planning, you can  make reservation with us at any time. The train tickets are sold out in minutes especially in travel peak, such as Chinese New Year. Book China train tickets, more early more better.
Q: When can I obtain the tickets?
A: The pre-sale period of China train tickets is 30 days prior to departure date for online booking (28 days for ticket office in advance) temporarily. The tickets for departure within the followings 30 days can be issued and confirmed immediately. The tickets for departure after the following 30 days are pre-booking. We will issue tickets on the first time when the train station releases them and provide final confirmation.
