After you made the full payment, we will send you the final confirmation letter after china train tickets issuing date, (4 -1 day ago usually in some Chinese travel peak date, like Spring Festival, Labor Day, National Day etc.)

5. For CTT Selling Price :
CTT selling price is based on the products you selected. If you select take the issued train tickets by yourself at train station or tickets selling offices on the street, you only need to pay for the ticket face price and insurance cost from CTT, which is USD 7 or RMB 42/ per ticket. If you want we delivery the tickets to your hotel address ( or specified private address ) in China, the cost is ticket price plus insurance cost which is USD 15 /per ticket or RMB 90 /per ticket. Delivery cost is USD 5 /time or RMB 30 /time. If you want us delivery to your home ( abroad of China ), insurance cost + international delivery by DHL or TNT, FedEx = USD 100/per time.

2, Train station to down town private transfer service: USD 30/per person. or RMB 183/per person.
including CTT sent out our driver meet you at exit of the train station and transfer you by private car (only for you and your families) to your hotel within downtown area. ( Not including luggage and porter services. )
3, Train tickets issue number Mobile Message Reminder Service: USD 5/per time or RMB 30/per time.
We will send you a mobile message with your issue number in it and this will be very convenient for you to get the ticket from the train station or ticket office show the clerk the tickets issue number in your mobile. You don't need to print out our final confirmation and take it by you.

8. For Late Bookings:
Bookings after China train tickets issuing date (20 days - 4 Day before your departure date, it depends on China Railway Bureau’s Order) We call it late bookings and it is hard to get these tickets, but we will try our best to help you. If we can’t get the train tickets finally, we will refund your full payment 24 hours ago before your train departure time. CTT will be not responsible for not get the tickets in the end. If the clients want to cancel the paid late bookings, he must inform us and confirm with us that we haven't get the tickets. If we haven't get the tickets, we will fully refund you the payment, if we have already get the tickets we can not refund nor change your tickets, but you can go to China Train Station to cancel it and get the 80% refund of the tickets face price before train departs. (80% refund is not CTT selling price, but the face price's. The lose will be on your own responsibility )



9. Cancellation Policy:
If CTT have not issued your train tickets (Usually it will be 20 - 12 Days ago before your departure date ), you can cancel at anytime and CTT will fully refund your payment. Cancellation made by clients within 7 days (168 hours) before departure time for final confirmed bookings, Clients can not get any refund from us. Since we have express delivered the tickets to your hotel and we can not get them back to cancel at train station in time, but you could get it and cancel at train station by yourself. There are different cancellation policies. If you cancel the tickets out of 48 hour before the train departure time, then you will get a 95% face price form the train authorities; if you cancel the tickets between 48 hours and 24 hours before the train departure time, then you can get a 90% face price form the train authorities; if you cancel the tickets with 24 hours before the train departure time, then you can get a 80% face price form the train authorities. The refund money will be going to our account first and you must let us know when the money come back we will refund the 80% face price money to your Paypal account. If you can not go to train station to cancel the tickets, we can send our guy to pick up it at your hotel and cancel it for you at train station, but it is USD 80 per time for the service. Service charge will be deducted from the refund. Cancellation made by clients out of 7 days (168 hours) for final confirmed bookings, the cancellation charge is 40% of CTT selling price, (Since our guys have to go to train stations to cancel your bookings ) you can get a 60% refund of the CTT selling price. Clients must provide the passport copies or scans for all passengers which are necessary to make the cancellation. If not provide above copies before 7 days, CTT can not refund any payment.

13. Children Tickets Policy
Domestic Children tickets Policy?
Domestic Children tickets standards: Children with 120-150 cm height need to buy the children tickets. Children with height under 120 cm can take the train free (sharing seats with parents). But every adult passenger could only take one child for this ticket free policy. For example, if you take two children, whose heights are both under 120 cm, ticket for one of them will be free but the other children need to buy the children tickets.
Domestic children ticket price: 60% of adult ticket price.

International train tickets Children tickets Policy?

Beijing to Moscow

International children ticket price: 70% of adult ticket price.
