China Railway News!

  • 【HOT】Train Tickets for May Day Holiday on Sale since Apr. 2, 2019
    Apr.2.2019

    This year, the May Day holiday starts from May 1 to 4. From April 2, 2019 on, the train tickets for the May Day has been on sale. According to the data of past years, there will be a travel peak in April 30 and May 1. Also, the tickets for return trip on May 4 are also very hot, which will be sold fromApril 5. It is estimated that there will be more travelers leaving for famous tourist destinations like Beijing, Xi'an, Chengdu, and Guilin during the 4-day holiday. Tourists plan to take a trip in this period are suggested to book train tickets as early as possible.
